About Me

Ebun majored in sciences on her GCSE with the desire to study orthopeadic surgery but after some soul searching she, fell in love with the IT world and took courses in html programming. Ebun has a passion for supporting women and disabled children; and she has done so by working with various charities in various countries. Ebun also loves to meet new people and she sings at her liesure time.
